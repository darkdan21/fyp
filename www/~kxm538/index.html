Research Interests

  • Interplay between interaction (information-flow) semantics and rewriting
    • Graph-rewriting abstract machines for functional programs, guided by Girard's Geometry of Interaction
    • Trade-off between space efficiency and time efficiency
    • Modelling run-time improvement of programs, as a simple extension of conventional functinal programming
