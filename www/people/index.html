Person Search

This directory lists staff, research students and visitors to the school who have opted to have their details listed. If a person has not completed the appropriate form their details are not be shown.

If you know the name of the person you are looking for, you can search for them here.

If no exact matches are found, the search uses a suitable Levenshtein distance to try to match names, so if you are unsure of spelling try giving your best guess at first and last name. For example, John Thomson would match Jon Thompson.

N.B. Members of the school can also use the full person directory (requires login).
