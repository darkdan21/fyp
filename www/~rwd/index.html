Reader in Intelligent Robotics

Research

My research is in the general area of reasoning under uncertainty. In particular, I work on planning and decision making, autonomy in robotics, and fault diagnosis. More detail about my research interests can be found here.

I am a P.I. for an EPSRC-funded project on sustained autonomy, funded through the Autonomous Intelligent Systems Programme. I have also been P.I. on the NERC-funded AFDA project on fault diagnosis for autonomous underwater vehicles and on two EU FP&-funded projects, CogX, which was concerned with building intelligent robots as office assistants, and GeRT, which involved learning planning operators from hand-built robot programs.

DX 2012: 23rd International Workshop on Principles of Diagnosis

I was the programme chair for DX 2012. The workshop website can be found here.
