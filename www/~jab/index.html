Research

My research is within Artificial Intelligence and has the following main aspects:

  1. the processing of FIGURATIVE UTTERANCES (metaphoric, metonymic, hyperbolic, ironic, ...)

  2. the nature of (SELF-)CONSCIOUSNESS, and how to achieve it in artefacts.

On the first of these, see the separate ATT-Meta project and Gen-Meta project pages.
