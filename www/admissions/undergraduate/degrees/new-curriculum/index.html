2018 Undergraduate Computer Science Curriculum

For 2018 entry we are introducing an exciting new curriculum for our undergraduate degrees. The aim is to provide our students with a wide range of in-depth options in the final year, each supported by prerequisites achieved in the first two years. In this new curriculum our students will study 120 credits worth of modules in each of the first two years and 80 credits of options and a 40 credit project in the final year. (For the MSci and MEng programmes, students will study 120 credits of options in 3rd year, and 60 credits of options and a 60 credit project in the final year.)

The BSc in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science is dedicated to the study of AI, and allows you to go much deeper into AI-module content.

New module information

  • BSc Computer Science with Year in Industry (G401)
  • BSc Computer Science with Study Abroad (I10A)

  • BSc Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science (GG47)

  • MSci Computer Science (I101)
  • MSci Computer Science with Year in Industry (I103)
  • MSci Computer Science with Study Abroad (I102)
  • MEng Computer Science (GG46)
