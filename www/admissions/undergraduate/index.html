Computer Science at Birmingham, UK

We offer a variety of undergraduate degree programmes at our UK campus, in disciplines allied to computer science, software engineering, and artificial intelligence:

  • BSc Computer Science
  • MSci Computer Science
  • BSc Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science
  • MEng Computer Science and Software Engineering
  • BSc Computer Science with Digital Technology Partnership with PwC - New Programme
  • Physical Sciences Foundation Year

Useful information

  • The BSc degrees are three year programmes
  • The MEng/MSci degrees are four year programmes. An MEng/MSci is an integrated Masters level qualification that provides an alternative to the traditional BSc + MSc route.
  • The BSc and MSci degrees in Computer Science are both available as a four year programme with a Year of Study Abroad
  • All of our undergraduate degrees are available with a Year in Industry
  • The school also offers an Year in Computer Science, which offers students the opportunity to take a one year break from their main degree programme to study a year in our School

Careers and Employability

Our School has strong local and national relationships with major industry players from the public and private sector, and 94% of our students go into graduate employment (DLHE Survey 2015/16). We have extensive student support services to help you refine your career options. We hold specialist careers fairs throughout the year and have a wide network of links that can help you to secure a year in industry, a placement and ultimately a graduate position.
